01 — The Problem

CRYPTO IS EVERYWHERE.
SPENDABLE NOWHERE.

USERS
  • No seamless way to spend crypto at checkout — holders are stuck converting before they can pay.
  • P2P conversion carries real fraud risk. Binance estimates P2P scam losses reached $3B in 2025.
  • Very few real-world places actually accept crypto payment today.
MERCHANTS
  • Most merchants avoid crypto entirely — the integration feels too complex.
  • Price volatility between sale and settlement is a real, unmanaged risk.
  • No accessible infrastructure exists for blockchain-powered commerce at the till.
02 — Why It Matters

REAL ADOPTION
NEEDS A REAL ON-RAMP.

Who it affects

Users can't spend USDC day-to-day without routing through P2P first.

Merchants lose customers because accepting crypto today feels risky.

Why people should care

Millions of crypto holders across Africa want to spend, not just hold. Today's tooling blocks real financial inclusion at the exact moment it's needed most.

Why now

Over 54 million Africans use crypto today. The region processed $200B+ in crypto transactions in 2025 driven by need, not hype.

03 — The Solution

ONE TAP.
INSTANT SETTLEMENT.

FOR USERS
  • Spend crypto instantly in real-world transactions, over Bluetooth.
  • Zero technical knowledge required. Pay the way you already pay with cash.
FOR MERCHANTS
  • Accept crypto with zero volatility exposure — settlement lands in local currency.
  • Instant settlement at the point of sale, no waiting on confirmations.
  • Accept payment via Bluetooth, QR code, or the Blink Crypto POS.
